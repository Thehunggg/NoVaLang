# bg — Native speaker review checklist

For a native Bulgarian (bg-BG) speaker. Simple tick-box items. Pure Bulgarian
language facts the project owner (not a Bulgarian speaker) cannot verify. None
block the current VALIDATED_NOT_YET_PROVEN status; they refine confidence.

No front-matter (native-review file, exempt).

---

## [N-bg-01] Gender assignment for lesson vocabulary

Confirm gender (masc/fem/neut) for the first lesson's nouns and any exceptions
not predictable from the ending.

- [ ] Gender correct for lesson-1 nouns.

## [N-bg-02] Definite article suffix forms

Confirm the postposed definite article, especially masculine -ът (subject) vs
-а (object) usage, and plural -те/-та.

- [ ] Definite suffix forms correct (incl. masc subject/object).

## [N-bg-03] Verb conjugation, aspect & evidential

Confirm present/aorist/imperfect forms, aspect pairs, the да-construction (no
infinitive), and the evidential -л (reported vs witnessed).

- [ ] Verb forms + aspect + evidential correct.

## [N-bg-04] Stress placement & vowel reduction

Confirm stress on the correct syllable and that unstressed vowel reduction
(а/ъ→[ɐ], о→[o~u]) sounds natural in audio.

- [ ] Stress + reduction correct for lesson vocabulary.

## [N-bg-05] Clitic pronoun placement

Confirm weak object pronouns (ме, те, го, ѝ, ми...) are placed correctly
relative to the verb in example sentences.

- [ ] Clitic placement correct.

## [N-bg-06] ти / Вие naturalness

Confirm the informal/formal choice reads naturally and господин/госпожа usage
is correct in every example.

- [ ] T-V choice natural; titles correct.

## [N-bg-07] Naturalness / register of translations

Per ADR-016: confirm sentences sound natural and contemporary, SVO order is
natural, and politeness formulas (моля, заповядайте) are idiomatic.

- [ ] Sentences natural; SVO + politeness idiomatic.
