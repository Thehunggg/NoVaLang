# ca — Native speaker review checklist

For a native Catalan (Central / IEC) speaker. Simple tick-box items. Pure
Catalan language facts the project owner (not a Catalan speaker) cannot verify.
None block the current VALIDATED_NOT_YET_PROVEN status; they refine confidence.

No front-matter (native-review file, exempt).

---

## [N-ca-01] Gender for common vocabulary

Gender (masc/fem) is largely lexical. Confirm the article (el/la) for the first
lesson's vocabulary.

- [ ] Gender + article correct for lesson-1 nouns.

## [N-ca-02] Apostrophe / contraction

Confirm elision (l'home, l'aigua), contractions (al, del, pel), and d' before
vowels are correct for lesson phrases.

- [ ] Apostrophe/contraction forms correct.

## [N-ca-03] Passat perifràstic

Confirm the periphrastic past (vaig/vas/va + infinitive) is used correctly and
sounds natural for lesson vocabulary.

- [ ] Passat perifràstic correct and natural.

## [N-ca-04] Weak pronouns (pronoms febles)

Confirm any weak-pronoun forms (em/et/el/hi/en, elided/reduced) are correct —
this is the hardest area.

- [ ] Weak-pronoun forms correct (if any appear).

## [N-ca-05] Vowel reduction in audio

Central Catalan reduces unstressed a/e→[ə], o/u→[u]. Confirm the audio reflects
this correctly for lesson words.

- [ ] Vowel reduction correct in audio.

## [N-ca-06] tu / vostè naturalness

Confirm tu/vostè usage is natural and vostè takes 3rd-person verb forms.

- [ ] tu/vostè natural; vostè + 3rd person correct.

## [N-ca-07] Central (not Valencian) + no castellanismes + naturalness

Per ADR-016: confirm sentences are natural Central Catalan (not Valencian
forms), avoid castellanismes (bueno/vale → bé/d'acord), adjective placement and
ç/l·l correct.

- [ ] Natural Central Catalan; no castellanismes; ç/l·l correct.
