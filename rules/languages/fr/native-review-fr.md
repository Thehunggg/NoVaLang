## C-06 — h aspiré vs h muet, and semi-vowel elision exceptions

The app plans to teach elision (l'ami, d'accord) but NOT apply it to
words starting with "h aspiré" (le héros, la haute, le hasard) or a small
group of vowel-spelled words pronounced with a semi-vowel (que oui, le
onze, le yaourt).
