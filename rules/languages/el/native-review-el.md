# el — Native speaker review checklist

For a native Modern Greek (el-GR) speaker. Simple tick-box items. Pure Greek
language facts the project owner (not a Greek speaker) cannot verify. None
block the current VALIDATED_NOT_YET_PROVEN status; they refine confidence.

No front-matter (native-review file, exempt).

---

## [N-el-01] Gender assignment for lesson vocabulary

Gender (ο/η/το) is partly predictable from endings but has many exceptions.
Confirm the article/gender for the first lesson's nouns.

- [ ] Gender + definite article correct for lesson-1 nouns.

## [N-el-02] Case forms (nom/gen/acc/voc)

Confirm the four case forms (singular + plural) for lesson nouns, articles,
and adjectives — especially genitive and any irregular declensions.

- [ ] Case paradigms correct for lesson vocabulary.

## [N-el-03] Verb conjugation & aspect

Confirm present/past/future forms, the imperfective↔perfective aspect pairs
(γράφω/γράψω), and irregular verbs (είμαι, έχω, πάω, λέω).

- [ ] Core verb tables + aspect pairs verified.

## [N-el-04] Stress placement (tonos)

Confirm the tonos is on the correct syllable for lesson words (stress can
distinguish meaning: πότε/ποτέ). Top priority — wrong stress changes the word.

- [ ] Stress/tonos correct for lesson vocabulary.

## [N-el-05] Synizesis & pronunciation edge cases

Confirm audio for synizesis words (άδεια → [ˈaðʝa]), palatalization
(γ/κ/χ before e/i), and αυ/ευ voicing ([av]/[af]).

- [ ] Audio pronunciation correct for edge cases.

## [N-el-06] εσύ / εσείς naturalness

Confirm the informal/formal choice reads as natural in each example (no
example uses εσύ where εσείς is socially expected, or vice versa).

- [ ] T-V choice natural in every example.

## [N-el-07] Naturalness / register of translations

Per ADR-016: confirm example sentences sound natural and contemporary (not
katharevousa-stiff), clitic placement correct (σε ευχαριστώ), article usage
natural (ο Γιάννης).

- [ ] Sentences natural; clitics + articles correct.
