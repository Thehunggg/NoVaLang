# hu — Native speaker review checklist

For a native Hungarian (hu-HU) speaker. Simple tick-box items. Pure Hungarian
language facts the project owner (not a Hungarian speaker) cannot verify. None
block the current VALIDATED_NOT_YET_PROVEN status; they refine confidence.

No front-matter (native-review file, exempt).

---

## [N-hu-01] Vowel harmony on suffixes

Suffixes take front/back (and rounding) variants. Confirm the harmonized suffix
form for the first lesson's words (-ban/-ben, -hoz/-hez/-höz).

- [ ] Vowel-harmony suffix forms correct.

## [N-hu-02] Case-suffix stacking

Confirm stacked suffixes (plural + possessive + case) are in the right order and
form for lesson vocabulary.

- [ ] Suffix stacking correct.

## [N-hu-03] Definite / indefinite conjugation

Confirm verbs use the correct conjugation (definite látom vs indefinite látok)
for the object definiteness in lesson sentences.

- [ ] Definite/indefinite conjugation correct.

## [N-hu-04] s / sz and vowel length in audio

Confirm audio: s=[ʃ], sz=[s], and short/long vowel distinctions (öt/őt) are
correct.

- [ ] Consonant + vowel length correct in audio.

## [N-hu-05] te / ön naturalness

Confirm te/ön usage is natural and ön/maga take 3rd-person verb forms; maga not
used where it could feel rude.

- [ ] te/ön natural; ön + 3rd person correct.

## [N-hu-06] Word order (topic/focus)

Confirm the focus-before-verb word order sounds natural for lesson sentences.

- [ ] Word order natural (focus placement).

## [N-hu-07] Naturalness / register of translations

Per ADR-016: confirm sentences sound natural, suffixes/harmony correct, no
foreign-structure interference.

- [ ] Sentences natural; morphology correct.
