# hr — Native speaker review checklist

For a native Croatian (hr-HR) speaker. Simple tick-box items. Pure Croatian
language facts the project owner (not a Croatian speaker) cannot verify. None
block the current VALIDATED_NOT_YET_PROVEN status; they refine confidence.

No front-matter (native-review file, exempt).

---

## [N-hr-01] Case forms for common vocabulary

7 cases × 3 genders × number. Confirm the case paradigm (esp. genitive,
locative, accusative) for the first lesson's nouns.

- [ ] Case forms correct for lesson-1 nouns.

## [N-hr-02] Verb aspect pairs

Perfective/imperfective is lexical (pisati/napisati). Confirm the aspect pair
and present-tense conjugation for lesson verbs.

- [ ] Aspect pairs + conjugation correct.

## [N-hr-03] Enclitic placement (Wackernagel)

Enclitics (sam/je/ga/se…) go in second position. Confirm word order in lesson
sentences is natural.

- [ ] Enclitic placement natural.

## [N-hr-04] Pitch accent

Croatian has a 4-way pitch accent (grȁd/grȃd). Confirm it is correct in audio
for lesson vocabulary where it matters.

- [ ] Pitch accent correct in audio.

## [N-hr-05] ti / Vi naturalness

Confirm ti/Vi usage is natural and Vi takes 2nd-person plural verb forms.

- [ ] ti/Vi natural; Vi + 2pl correct.

## [N-hr-06] Croatian (not Serbian/Bosnian) vocabulary

Confirm lesson vocabulary is Croatian standard (kruh, tisuća, tjedan — not
hljeb, hiljada, sedmica) and no Serbisms/Bosnianisms slipped in.

- [ ] Croatian standard vocabulary; no sr/bs mixing.

## [N-hr-07] Naturalness / register of translations

Per ADR-016: confirm sentences sound natural, case usage correct, no
article-language interference.

- [ ] Sentences natural; cases + word order correct.
