# uk — Native speaker review checklist

For a native Ukrainian (uk-UA) speaker. Simple tick-box items. Pure Ukrainian
language facts the project owner (not a Ukrainian speaker) cannot verify. None
block the current VALIDATED_NOT_YET_PROVEN status; they refine confidence.

No front-matter (native-review file, exempt).

---

## [N-uk-01] Gender assignment for lesson vocabulary

Gender is partly predictable from endings but has exceptions. Confirm gender +
any animacy effects for the first lesson's nouns.

- [ ] Gender + animacy correct for lesson-1 nouns.

## [N-uk-02] Case forms — all 7 including vocative

Confirm the seven case forms (singular + plural), especially the **vocative**
(Петре!, Оксано!, друже!) and any stem changes (стіл→стола, ніч→ночі).

- [ ] Case paradigms + vocative correct.

## [N-uk-03] Verb conjugation & aspect

Confirm present/past/future, aspect pairs (читати/прочитати), past-tense
gender agreement, and the synthetic future (читатиму).

- [ ] Verb tables + aspect + synthetic future verified.

## [N-uk-04] Stress placement (mobile)

Confirm stress on the correct syllable for lesson words and their inflected
forms (mobile stress: рука́ → ру́ки). Top priority — not marked in text.

- [ ] Stress correct for lesson vocabulary + forms.

## [N-uk-05] Palatalization & apostrophe

Confirm soft/hard consonants and apostrophe words (об'єкт, п'ять, сім'я) sound
correct in audio (' = [j] + hard consonant, not palatalized).

- [ ] Palatalization + apostrophe pronunciation correct.

## [N-uk-06] ти / ви naturalness + vocative

Confirm the informal/formal choice reads naturally and vocative address is
correct (пане Петре, not пан Петро) in every example.

- [ ] T-V choice + vocative natural.

## [N-uk-07] Naturalness / no русизми

Per ADR-016: confirm sentences sound natural and contemporary, use standard
Ukrainian words (дякую, будь ласка), and contain no unwanted russianisms.

- [ ] Sentences natural; no русизми; standard Ukrainian.
